Lasith Malinga



Full Name: Separamadu Laisth Malinga Swarnajith

Born: 28 August 1983


    Lasith Malinga is a very famous former Sri Lankan international cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest youcker bowlers.
Due to his popularity, he has become known among the world community as Slinga.He is so popular because of his bowling style. He is also the highest wicket-taker in an IPL match. Lasith Malinga is also the only player in the world to take four wickets twice in four balls. Under Malinga's leadership, the Sri Lankan team was able to win many matches. In 2019, Malinga also became the first player to reach 100 wickets in a Twenty20 match.

      Despite being at such a great level today, Malinga's childhood was a bitter one. His birthplace is Rathgama village in the Galle area. He has been a fan of cricket since he was a child. His first team and playground were his neighbors and the village coconut grove. He came to Cricket Ground in 2004 and retired in 2020.
